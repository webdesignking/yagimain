About YAGI

YAGI (Young and Getting It) is a subsidiary of the GODSIGNS ACADEMY geared at empowering Young people with knowledge profitable for a fulfilled life. The GODSIGNS ACADEMY is an organization with the sole aim of youth empowerment with diverse skills (both digital and manual skills) YAGI is founded by Gospel Azubuike Odinakachukwu as a platform for creation of a better society habitable for the younger generation. in YAGI we deal with youth and emotions, youth and wealth, youth and leadership, youth and God, youth and skills, and lots more. YAGI has the vision to empower young men and women through knowledge to help them live in fulfilment and is on the mission to curb problems with the youth and society creating all round balanced youth.
